Full job description

We are seeking a motivated individual to join our team as an Office Assistant. The ideal candidate must be motivated, reliable, energetic, can multi-task, prioritize, and have a positive attitude.

Responsibilities include, but are not limited to, processing and monitoring the progress of quotations and orders received from our customers and suppliers, ensure that it is all processed in a timely manner, respond to customer and supplier emails, and report all issues to management.

This is a full-time position Monday- Friday 6:30am- 3:00pm.

The work environment is business casual.

We also provide benefits such as health insurance, paid time off, and vacation pay.
